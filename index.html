About Us



"Everyone can tell you the risk, but only an entrepreneur can see the reward”

Novatia is built by BITSians. To ensure it was for BITSians as well, and as a way to give back to our institute, we launched the BITSian Track. To participate in Novatia through the BITSian Track, the startup must have atleast one co-founder who is either a student or alumni of BITS Pilani (any campus).
The BITSian must be actively involved in the venture for it to be eligible for the BITSian Track. We select 10 BITSian startups exclusively for the mentorship programme, out of which 3 are guaranteed to make it to the finale. In the event that the startup is selected through the Genera Track, but is eligible for the BITSian Track, they must declare the choice they wish to pursue.
We encourage all BITSians to present their ideas and register for Novatia '21.

Who should participate


Novatia invites all startups & entrepreneurs who intend on taking the world by storm with their business.
We look for startups that inspire others through their success and stand as a beacon of innovation.
We focus on early-stage ventures, looking to take the next big leap in the entrepreneurial journey and grow their businesses.
We encourage originality, innovation and ideas that build a whole new status quo.

Timeline


Registration [Sept. 1 to Nov. 30]

We receive registrations of over 500+ startups from across India.
The top 50 startups are offered the opportunity to receive mentorship from India’s most accomplished leaders during Mentorship Sessions.
To provide equal opportunity to all startups, registrations take place via an F6S form, where questions are tailored by investors and take a rigorous approach to thoroughly validate and vet every single startup idea.

Mentorship [Dec. 14 to Jan. 13]

Our mentors form one of the most essential part of our competition. They will actively engage with the startups through online conversations to guide them through the numerous possible challenges they might face.

Coalescence [Jan. 23]

Coalescence is the flagship seminar, organised by CEL. This year, we intend to incorporate it with Novatia, and in doing so we plan on taken out E-Summit to newer heights.

Pitch Day [Jan. 24]

The startups selected for the finale are given the opportunity to present their ideas to a panel of esteemed leaders, entrepreneurs and investors from every field of life.
Our jury then selects a winner from the pitches, who is presented with the title of Emerging Business Leader, Novatia ’21 along with the prizes
